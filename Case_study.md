CASE STUDY – TASKFLOW

Project Overview

Taskflow is an AI-powered productivity mobile application designed to help users organize tasks, improve focus, and achieve goals efficiently. The app combines task management with AI assistance to provide a seamless and engaging productivity experience.

────────────────────────────────

Problem Statement

Many users struggle with managing daily tasks, maintaining productivity, and staying organized. Existing productivity apps often feel overwhelming, cluttered, or lack personalization.

The goal of Taskflow was to design a modern, intuitive, and visually engaging mobile application that helps users manage tasks while leveraging AI assistance to improve productivity.

────────────────────────────────

Research Process

Secondary Research

Research was conducted on existing productivity and task management applications such as:

• Notion
• Todoist
• TickTick
• Microsoft To Do
• Google Tasks

Key Findings

• Users prefer simple onboarding experiences.
• Dark mode is increasingly popular among younger users.
• AI-powered assistance is becoming a key differentiator.
• Quick task creation improves usability.
• Visual hierarchy improves navigation and engagement.

Challenges Faced

Visual Balance

Creating a dark interface while maintaining readability required careful contrast adjustments.

Branding

Finding a balance between a professional productivity tool and a visually appealing AI product was challenging.

Consistency

Ensuring consistent spacing, typography, and component styling across screens required repeated refinement.

────────────────────────────────

Design Decisions

Dark Mode First

Dark mode was selected to reduce eye strain and align with modern design trends.

Purple Accent Theme

Purple was chosen to communicate creativity, innovation, and AI-driven functionality.

Rounded Components

Rounded corners improve visual friendliness and enhance usability.

Minimal Layout

A clean interface helps users focus on productivity rather than unnecessary visual distractions.

────────────────────────────────

Accessibility Improvements

Several accessibility considerations were incorporated:

• High contrast text against dark backgrounds
• Large touch targets for buttons
• Consistent navigation patterns
• Clear visual hierarchy
• Readable typography sizes
• Simple onboarding process

────────────────────────────────

Future Improvements

Future versions of Taskflow could include:

• AI-generated task recommendations
• Voice command integration
• Calendar synchronization
• Team collaboration features
• Progress analytics dashboard
• Personalized productivity insights
• Cross-platform support
• Smart reminders and notifications

────────────────────────────────

Conclusion

Taskflow was designed to simplify productivity management through a clean user experience and AI-powered assistance. By combining modern visual design principles with usability-focused decisions, the application delivers an engaging and accessible productivity solution for students and professionals alike.
